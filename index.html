About

Outside-The-Box Instruments are a pleasure for eye, hand, and ear. Each is unique and handcrafted with care.

Instruments begin with vibrant artwork created in acrylic paint with water-based finish.

Fretted diatonically, these instruments present only the notes of D or G Major, perfect for the beginner or musician seeking creative inspiration. Chromatic fretting is available on request.

Built of sustainable woods, their small body and short-scale neck make Outside-The-Box Instruments easy to play. Each is ergonomically balanced for comfort.

Outside-The-Box Instruments make beautiful music and enhance the look of every room.
Custom commissions are accepted.
