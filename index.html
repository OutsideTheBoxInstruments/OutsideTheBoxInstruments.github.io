About

Outside-The-Box Instruments are a pleasure to the eye, hand, and ear. All are handcrafted and each one unique.

Instruments begin with vibrant artwork in acrylic paint, sealed with water-based finish.

Made of sustainable woods, the small body and short-scale neck's low-tension strings make an Outside-The-Box Instrument easy to play. Choose from diatonic (dulcimer) or standard chromatic tunings.

Commissions for custom instruments are accepted.
